Top Three Activities To Do in New York

Get some Street Food

New York has some of the gresies, tastiest street food in the US of A so make sure you wait in line and get a hot dog, gyro, or snack!

Go Hiking on our Beautiful Mountains

New York is often not known for it's beautiful Mountains but the Adirondack Mountains north of the City will definitely have you craving more nature!

Go see our plethora of Lighthouses

Surprisingly New York has a lot of Lighthouses, and a lot of beautiful ones at that, which hold a ton of beautiful history. Don't forget about them!
